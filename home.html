What We Do

    At NouSante, we are committed to revolutionizing healthcare in Mauritius through innovative technology solutions. 
    Recognizing the challenges faced by public hospitals in managing patient data and appointments, we have developed 
    a state-of-the-art eHealth platform. Our platform aims to streamline medical processes, enhance patient care, and 
    support sustainable development goals.
  
    Our mission is to leverage the power of computer science to create a robust eHealth ecosystem that connects 
    patients, physicians, and healthcare providers seamlessly. By digitizing medical records and appointment systems, 
    we aim to eliminate paperwork, improve efficiency, and ensure better healthcare outcomes for all Mauritians.
